About Us

The Church of Blood and Water exists to glorify God and help people grow in faith. Our name comes from John 19:34, where blood and water flowed from the side of Christ. We see these symbols as reminders of redemption, renewal, and hope.

We welcome Christians from Protestant, Catholic, Orthodox, and non-denominational backgrounds. Our goal is not to erase traditions but to unite believers around Jesus Christ.

We value worship, prayer, Scripture study, compassion, and community life. Visitors are always welcome.
